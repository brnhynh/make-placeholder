# Contributing Guidelines

Thank you for your interest in contributing to **make-placeholder**. We appreciate your help in making this project better.

## How Can You Contribute?

There are several ways you can contribute to the project:

1. **Reporting Issues**: If you encounter a bug or have a suggestion, please [open an issue](https://github.com/brnhynh/make-placeholder/issues). Make sure to provide detailed information to help us understand and address the problem.

2. **Feature Requests**: If you have ideas for new features, feel free to open an issue to discuss your proposal. We value your input and will consider your suggestions.

3. **Pull Requests**: If you want to contribute code or documentation changes, please submit a pull request (PR). Here's a general process for creating PRs:
   - Fork the repository.
   - Create a new branch for your changes.
   - Make your changes and ensure that they pass tests (if applicable).
   - Submit a pull request with a clear description of your changes.

## Licensing

By contributing to this project, you agree that your contributions will be licensed under the project's MIT License. Make sure you have the necessary rights to provide your contributions under this license.

## Thank You

We appreciate your contributions and value your time and effort in helping us improve **make-placeholder**. Your participation in this open-source project is essential to its success.

If you have any questions or need clarification on any guidelines, please feel free to reach out.
